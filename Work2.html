Code

Abstract

The COVID-19 pandemic has significantly impacted societies worldwide, underscoring the need for effective tracking and monitoring tools. In response, this abstract presents an Android application, COVID-19 Tracker, designed to provide real-time updates, analysis, and assistance during the ongoing pandemic. The application leverages data from reputable sources such as the World Health Organization (WHO), national health agencies, and local authorities to ensure accurate and up-to-date information.
The COVID-19 Tracker app offers several key features to help users navigate the pandemic. Firstly, it provides a real-time global map, enabling users to visualize the spread of the virus at both macro and micro levels. The map displays active cases, recoveries, and fatalities, allowing users to monitor the situation in their locality or any desired region. Additionally, the app offers comprehensive statistical data such as the number of confirmed cases, hospitalizations, testing rates, and vaccination progress.
